Poem from a favourite movie

Zindagi Na Milegi Dobara

From "Zindagi Na Milegi Dobara," here's a famous 4-line poem:
"Dilon mein tum apni
Betaabiyan leke chal rahe ho
Toh zinda ho tum
Nazar mein khwaabon ki bijliyan leke chal rahe ho"

Translation:

"If you carry impatience in your hearts
Then you are alive
If you carry lightning bolts of dreams in your eyes
Then you are alive"
